执行命令

前提

  • 已设置好配置项：系统配置、认证配置、主机配置

执行方式：普通用户

单条：

$ xsh.exe -mode cmd -group alpha02 -cmd whoami
[192.168.165.37    ] ---------------------------------------------------------
abc

[192.168.170.109   ] ---------------------------------------------------------
abc

多条：

$ xsh.exe -mode cmd -group alpha02 -cmd whoami;date
[192.168.170.109   ] ---------------------------------------------------------
abc
Tue May  5 13:58:42 CST 2020

[192.168.165.37    ] ---------------------------------------------------------
abc
Tue May  5 13:58:33 CST 2020

多条（含空格）：

$ xsh.exe -mode cmd -group alpha02 -cmd "whoami; date; free -h"
[192.168.170.109   ] ---------------------------------------------------------
abc
Tue May  5 14:01:04 CST 2020
              total        used        free      shared  buff/cache   available
Mem:            15G        2.6G        2.7G         34M         10G         12G
Swap:            0B          0B          0B

[192.168.165.37    ] ---------------------------------------------------------
abc
Tue May  5 14:00:55 CST 2020
              total        used        free      shared  buff/cache   available
Mem:           7.6G        1.0G        1.1G        449M        5.5G        5.9G
Swap:            0B          0B          0B
